Frequently Asked Questions

Is my money safe?

Yes. All GIC products offered through Customplan GIC are from CDIC (Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation) or provincially insured financial institutions. This ensures 100% of your principal and interest is protected up to insurance limits.

What is CDIC insurance coverage?

CDIC is a federal Crown corporation that protects eligible deposits up to $100,000 per insured category at each member institution. For credit unions and other provincial institutions, similar coverage is provided through provincial bodies like CUDIC in BC.

Are there any fees for your service?

No. We provide a completely NO FEE service for our clients. We are compensated by the financial institutions, allowing you to benefit from the highest rates without any out-of-pocket costs.

How do you offer higher rates than my bank?

Through our partnership with GICDirect, we have access to more than 40 financial institutions across Canada. Smaller banks and trust companies often offer much higher rates to attract new deposits compared to the Big 5 banks.

What types of accounts can I open?

We offer GICs for Non-Registered (Cash), RRSP, TFSA, RRIF, RESP, and Corporate accounts.
